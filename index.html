IT Assets Disposition

IT asset disposition (ITAD) is a best in class business practice that provide financial, contractual and inventory functions to support life cycle management and strategic decision making for the IT environment. Assets include all legacy Telecommunications equipment, Personal Computers, Ethernet Switches and Routers that are found in the business environment.

Get in touch

Shoot us an email or give us a call. We'll get an idea for your project requirements and our team of experts will be more than happy to discuss your needs and schedule an on-site visit to develop a disposal strategy.

On site visit

The on-site visit will allow us to survey the equipment that is being considered for disposal. Upon completion of the survey KingWorks3 will provide you with an audit of the equipment and a Scope of Work (SOW) will be generated to identify the steps required to complete the project.

Asset pick-up & removal

Once the SOW has been accepted our ITAD team will assist you with disposing of your legacy hardware/software platforms utilizing the following process:

  • KW3 will arrange for the collection and secure transportation of the materials identified in the SOW
  • KW3 will receive the material at our secure facilities and provide a full serialized audit of the equipment received
  • KW3 will identify any of the equipment that can be resold or redeployed back the customer after it has been tested, refurbished, and audited if applicable

Data wiped and devices disposed

After ensuring the devices are safely stored and inspected we will help you through the last and one of the most important steps, completely destroying your data:

  • KW3 is responsible for the data wiping and destruction of information carrying devices
  • KW3 is responsible for providing R2 Certified Recycling or a Certificate of destruction (where applicable)
  • KW3 will provide a settlement within 45 days. A credit will be issued for any equipment that is identified as eligible for re-sale

Unified Communications as a Service

UCaaS refers to a phone system that uses Voice over Internet Protocol to provide a Cloud based phone system for businesses. Often this service is provided by a network service provider as part of an overall network solution. Enjoy ultimate flexibility as access to “seats” are typically purchased on a monthly basis and can scale up and down as needed. There’s none of the capital equipment or hardware costs associated with legacy phone systems.
