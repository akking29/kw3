UCaaS SOLUTIONS

Communication technology aimed at improving employee collaboration. Enjoy ultimate flexibility as access to “seats” are typically purchased on a monthly basis and can scale up and down as needed. There’s none of the capital equipment or hardware costs associated with legacy phone systems.

How does a UCaaS system work?

Hosted Infrastructure

The hosted infrastructure required to provide UC functions (chat, presence, etc.) resides in the data center of your service provider.

Resides in Cloud

Your IP connection to the cloud allows users to access the data center & functionality.

Main Features

UCaaS refers to a phone system that uses Voice over Internet Protocol to provide a Cloud based phone system for businesses. Often this service is provided by a network service provider as part of an overall network solution.
